Description

Simple and tasty, these peanut butter bars are sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

Base:

  • 1 pound powdered sugar
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 cups peanut butter

Topping:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Directions

Base:

  1. Mix powdered sugar, butter, graham crackers crumbs, and peanut butter in a bowl.
  2. Spread out in an even layer in a 9x13 pan.

Topping:

  1. Melt butter.
  2. Add chocolate chips and melt.
  3. Pour on top of the peanut butter mixture.
  4. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
  5. Cut then return to refrigerator until time to serve.
