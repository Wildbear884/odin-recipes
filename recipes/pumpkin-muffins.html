Description:

These muffins are light and fluffy and the streusel topping adds a perfect bit of sweet crunch.
They are great for a light breakfast.

Ingredients:

Wet Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup canned pumpkin
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons molasses

Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon allspice
  • 1/8 teaspoon cloves
  • 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Streusel Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons rolled oats
  • 2 tablespoons butter, room temp
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:

  1. Set oven to 375Â°F
  2. Prepare a muffin pan with muffin pan liners.
  3. In a large bowl whisk together the oil, sugar, and brown sugar.
  4. Then beat in the eggs, pumpkin, buttermilk, and molasses.
  5. In another bowl, mix the dry ingredients together.
  6. In a third bowl, mix the streusel topping together, using your hands to cut in the butter until it is thoroughly combined and crumbly.
  7. Mix the dry ingredients into the wet, stirring just enough to combine.
  8. Evenly pour the batter into the prepared muffin pan.
  9. Sprinkle the streusel topping evenly over the muffins.
  10. Bake for about 20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  11. Let them cool for a few minutes in the pan before taking the out.
