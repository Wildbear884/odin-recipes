Description

This recipe is easy to make and tastes delicious!
With it's simple seasoning and easy prep this dish is a wonderful side dish for almost any meat and potatoes dish.

Ingredients

  • 6 small red potatoes, cleaned and cut into 1/2 to 1 inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, toss the potatoes with the oil and spices.
  2. Spray a shallow baking pan with cooking spray.
  3. Spread the potatoes in the bottom of the prepared pan.
  4. Bake at 450Â°F for 25-30 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.
  5. Stir the potatoes once after about 15 minutes of baking so they brown on all sides.
